# Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations - Modules Overview

This document provides a comprehensive overview of all available modules in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations, sourced from Microsoft Learn documentation.

---

## Core Finance & Operations Apps

Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations is a suite of enterprise resource planning (ERP) software-as-a-service (SaaS) offerings built on Microsoft Azure. The suite includes the following main solution areas:

### 1. **Dynamics 365 Finance**
A comprehensive financial management solution that empowers organizations to manage global financial systems with accuracy and insight.

#### Finance Sub-Modules:
| Module | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **General Ledger** | Core accounting structure including chart of accounts, financial dimensions, and accounting structure |
| **Accounts Payable** | Managing accounting processes for paying vendors and suppliers for goods or services received |
| **Accounts Receivable** | Managing accounting processes for receiving payments for goods and services delivered |
| **Cash and Bank Management** | Managing cash flows, bank reconciliation, and treasury operations |
| **Budgeting** | Budget planning, forecasting, and budget control capabilities |
| **Fixed Assets** | Tracking, managing, and deprecating fixed asset inventory |
| **Cost Accounting** | Collecting and analyzing cost data from various sources for management decision-making |
| **Expense Management** | Employee expense tracking and reimbursement processes |
| **Project Management** | Project-specific accounting and financial tracking |
| **Public Sector** | Specialized functionality for public sector organizations |
| **Compliance** | Audit policies, regulatory compliance, and policy enforcement |

---

### 2. **Dynamics 365 Supply Chain Management**
A unified solution providing end-to-end visibility of inventory, warehouse, manufacturing, service, and logistics capabilities.

#### Supply Chain Sub-Modules:
| Module | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Inventory Management** | Managing inventory levels, movements, and warehouse stock |
| **Warehouse Management** | Configuration and management of warehouse operations |
| **Master Planning** | Demand forecasting and production planning |
| **Demand Planning** | AI-powered demand forecasting and planning optimization |
| **Procurement and Sourcing** | Managing purchase orders, vendor relationships, and procurement processes |
| **Product Information Management (PIM)** | Managing product data, attributes, and product hierarchies |
| **Engineering Change Management** | Managing product engineering and design changes through lifecycle |
| **Production Control** | Managing production orders, batch orders, and kanban processes |
| **Cost Management** | Analyzing and optimizing supply chain costs |
| **Sales and Marketing** | Managing sales orders, quotations, and marketing processes |
| **Transportation Management** | Optimizing shipping, freight, and logistics operations |
| **Service Management** | Managing after-sales service operations and service delivery |
| **Landed Cost** | Tracking and managing landed costs for imported goods |
| **Rebate Management** | Managing vendor and customer rebate programs |
| **Asset Management** | Managing operational assets and maintenance |
| **Sensor Data Intelligence** | AI-powered insights from IoT sensor data (preview) |
| **Unified Pricing Management** | Centralized pricing strategy management |

## Business Process Scenarios

Finance & Operations apps support the following end-to-end business process scenarios:

1. **Acquire to Dispose** - Asset lifecycle management
2. **Design to Retire** - Product lifecycle from design to end-of-life
3. **Forecast to Plan** - Demand forecasting and planning
4. **Inventory to Deliver** - Inventory management and order fulfillment
5. **Order to Cash** - Sales process from order to payment
6. **Plan to Produce** - Production planning and manufacturing
7. **Project to Profit** - Project implementation and profitability
8. **Service to Deliver** - Service delivery operations
9. **Source to Pay** - Procurement process from sourcing to payment
10. **Record to Report** - Financial recording and reporting

## Deployment Models

### Cloud Deployment
- **Hosted on Microsoft Azure**
- **SaaS model with continuous updates**
- **Recommended for most organizations**

### On-Premises Deployment
- **Deployed in customer data centers**
- **Greater control over infrastructure**
- **Available for specialized requirements**

## Integration Capabilities

Finance & Operations apps integrate with:
- **Microsoft Office 365** (Word, Excel, Outlook, Teams)
- **Microsoft Power Platform** (Power Apps, Power Automate, Power BI)
- **Azure Services** (Data Factory, Synapse Analytics, etc.)
- **Dataverse** (via Dual-Write and Virtual Entities)
- **Third-party applications** (via APIs and connectors)
